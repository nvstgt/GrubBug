A07-2021: Identification and Authentication Failures

Identification and Authentication Failures occur when systems fail to properly confirm the identity of a user or verify their access credentials, potentially allowing unauthorized access to sensitive data or actions.

Examples

  • Weak Password Policies
  • Session Fixation Attacks
  • Lack of Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
